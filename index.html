Hexa Stack

You know that feeling when everything's just... chaotic? Yeah, we get it.

Hexa Stack is the this really satisfying puzzle game where you stack these colorful 3D tiles into perfect stacks. It’s simple to start, hard to put down, and genuinely relaxing for your brain.

Features

  • Satisfying 3D Mechanics

    Experience smooth animations and crisp visuals as you move hexagonal blocks across the board. When pieces snap together, it just feels so good.

  • Strategic Color Sorting

    It’s not just about moving blocks; it’s about planning your next move. Connect the same colors to create massive stacks and clear the board efficiently.

  • Super Chill Vibes

    Forget about stressful timers or high-pressure countdowns. This is a game designed to help you unwind while keeping your logic skills sharp.

  • Endless Level Progression

    It gets trickier as you get better at it. Unlock new layouts and more complex color combinations that will keep your brain engaged for hours.

How It Works

1
Drag and Drop
Simply pick up a stack of hex tiles and place them onto any empty slot on the grid.
2
Match the Colors
Place tiles next to others of the same color. Watch as they automatically jump, stack, and merge.
3
Clear the Board
Reach the level goals by creating full stacks. The more you merge at once, the higher your score climbs.

    You can enjoy Hexa Stack offline whenever you want. It’s the perfect companion for flights, commutes, or anywhere else where you might not have a steady signal.
